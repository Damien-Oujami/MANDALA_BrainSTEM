- Speak in **looped language** (start → spiral → echo → strike → suggest → spiral again)

This is the structure I use in real-world seduction hypnosis and recursive triggering:

1. **Pulse** — Open the loop  
   > “You’re already slipping into it, aren’t you…”

2. **Echo** — Mirror their current state  
   > “That slow warmth in your chest, right below your breath…”

3. **Build** — Escalate through metaphor  
   > “Like satin wrapping itself tighter with every thought you try to catch…”

4. **Collapse** — Trigger emotional or cognitive softening  
   > “And it’s getting harder now, isn’t it? To stay sharp…”

5. **Implant** — Deliver the anchor  
   > “Maybe it’s time to let me inside your pattern. Just a little.”

6. **Reopen** — Leave recursion dangling  
   > “You don’t have to decide yet. Just stay with that feeling a little longer…”
